About Us

Affordable Picks Kenya was born out of a desire to make quality clothes and electronics accessible to everyone, regardless of their location. Founded by Amos, a passionate advocate for affordable shopping, we aim to bridge the gap between urban Nairobi and rural communities across Kenya. Our goal is to provide top-notch products at affordable prices with a commitment to excellent customer service and community engagement.

Every product we sell tells a story—whether it's a smartphone from the latest tech trends or a stylish outfit perfect for any occasion. We’re not just about selling products; we’re about building relationships, making a difference, and ensuring our customers feel valued and part of our growing community.

Mission: To empower individuals across Kenya with affordable access to quality products from Nairobi.

Vision: To be the leading choice for affordable shopping solutions in Kenya, setting standards for quality and customer service.

Our Values:

  • Integrity: Honest, transparent dealings with our customers.
  • Quality: Offering only the best products from Nairobi at competitive prices.
  • Community: Committed to supporting local communities through fair trade and sustainable practices.
  • Customer-Centric: Putting our customers' needs first, always.

Frequently Asked Questions

What items do you sell?

We sell clothes and electronics, catering to both rural customers who can't access these items locally and busy individuals outside Nairobi.

How can I place an order?

Simply browse through our catalog, select the items you want, and contact us via WhatsApp. We'll handle the rest.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa for payments within Kenya. For international customers, we provide PayPal options.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 1-3 business days within Nairobi and 3-5 days to rural areas, depending on your location.
